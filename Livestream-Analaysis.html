First Livestream Analysis

Group 6

01

Summary of Stream

A recent livestream hosted by researchers studying the impact of the COVID-19 pandemic on the social skills of Grade 10-AM students at APEC Schools Tondo went beyond just presenting data. While the research focuses on the experiences of students, the researchers recognized the value of sharing their insights on navigating social interactions during various learning modalities.

Instead of focusing solely on the interviews with the 33 students planned for the research, the livestream offered a "watch-worthy" experience by turning the tables and interviewing the researchers themselves. This unique approach yielded valuable information not only for the audience but also for the researchers, as it provided them with additional data for their study titled "The Impact of The COVID-19 Pandemic on The Social Skills of Grade 10-AM Students At APEC Schools Tondo."

The live stream wasn't just informative, it was also engaging. Beyond the research-driven interview segment, the researchers included an entertaining segment featuring humorous hand games with "makeup consequences" for the losers. They also acknowledged the audience through shout-outs and commendations and even welcomed other livestreaming groups to share a few words before closing the stream.

This innovative format resulted in a well-rounded live stream that catered to both the research and the audience. It offered insights into the researchers' experiences, provided an entertaining segment, and fostered collaboration with other groups, leaving the audience informed and engaged.

02

Things Done Well

During the live stream itself, the things that were commendable among the things done were having a very comfortable atmosphere that offered a balance of entertainment, insight, and engagement.

With the livestream offering a comfortable atmosphere it allowed the researchers to open up regarding areas or points in their educational experience that may be sensitive or too personal for them. Just as one of the researchers showcased, they talked about points of their lives where they were not able to form friendships with their peers at all due to the restrictions offered by a full online learning modality.

Aside from this, having a very comfortable environment allowed engagement and entertaining interactions via mini-games in between the stream. It allowed proper sportsmanship amongst the researchers and opened their conversations to the other group's live streaming as well.

Another commendable aspect was the preparation of the technological aspect. The template, backups, camera, mic, lighting, logo, and so on. These were commendable because they were consistent with each other and gave the right meaning to the chosen content type.

During the livestream, when technical difficulties were met. The backups and operation were already prepared and the delay was very minimal. This means that preparations have taken place and have served their proper purpose.  

03

Areas for Opportunities

While the recent livestream enjoyed considerable positive aspects, the researchers identified several areas where future efforts could further enhance the experience and attract a wider audience.

To ensure a consistently stable internet connection, they could explore options beyond a mobile hotspot within a closed space and potentially invest in a more reliable connection or alternative streaming locations. Additionally, implementing a more robust pre-stream marketing strategy on platforms like social media, utilizing teasers, trailers, and engaging posts two weeks prior, could significantly increase viewership and excitement.

Furthermore, optimizing the chat box to display real-time Facebook comments would facilitate a smooth and interactive experience for both researchers and viewers. Finally, clear communication strategies, such as designated signals or alerts, would ensure the researchers are aware of the stream's start time, eliminating unnecessary dead air at the beginning. To add to this, the researchers should also watch their language and keep a more professional atmosphere as not to joke around too much with the audience.

By addressing these areas, the researchers can build upon the success of the initial livestream and create even more engaging and impactful experiences for future broadcasts. 

04

Level of Engagement

The recent livestream exceeded expectations, fostering a vibrant and engaged community around the channel. Viewer participation skyrocketed, with the number of comments doubling the anticipated amount to a staggering 19. This surge, alongside 2 shares and 14 reactions, signifies a captivating and impactful presentation that resonated deeply with the audience.

Beyond the raw numbers, the livestream's success stemmed from strategically implemented elements. Engaging games provided viewers with an avenue for active participation, creating a sense of community and shared experience. Additionally, thoughtful shoutouts acknowledged and appreciated viewers, fostering a sense of connection and encouraging further engagement.

Because of the game segments including that of the games or challenges done by the researchers using makeup materials followed by the shout outs done as well, these activities caused a boost in the engagement of the livestream among the audience and the livestreamers, that is the researchers.

The combined force of these strategies not only captivated existing viewers but also extended the reach far beyond the initial audience base. The lively discussions in the comments section, coupled with the reach generated by shares and reactions, created a positive ripple effect. This effect attracted new viewers through the networks of those who actively engaged with the stream, significantly expanding the audience base.

This resounding success serves as a valuable learning experience for future endeavors. By building upon this momentum and further refining these successful strategies, future live streams can connect with viewers on an even deeper level and solidify a thriving online community around the page. 

05

Viewership

The recent livestream offered valuable insights into the relationship between content and viewership. Initially, viewership remained low during segments lacking interactive elements or direct audience engagement. This suggests that passive content might not be as effective in captivating viewers compared to content that actively solicits their participation.

However, a significant viewership surge occurred during two specific segments: the "makeup consequences" portion of the mini-games and the dedicated shoutout sections. These segments incorporated elements that directly engaged viewers. The interactive nature of the mini-games, with potential consequences based on audience interaction, likely drew viewers in and encouraged them to stay tuned. Publicly acknowledging viewers by name in the shoutouts fostered a sense of community and appreciation, keeping them engaged and invested in the stream.

This observed correlation between viewership and engagement highlights the importance of incorporating interactive elements and consistently engaging with the audience throughout the live stream. By prioritizing engagement and fostering a sense of community, during future livestreams, the researchers can not only retain viewers but also actively expand their reach.

Leveraging these insights, the researchers can create captivating and interactive experiences that resonate deeply with their audience and cultivate a thriving online community. 

     

       

       

Second Livestream Analysis

Group 6

01

Summary of Stream

Recently, the researchers of the paper titled "The Impact of COVID-19 Pandemic on Grade 10-AM Students’ Social Skills in Blended Learning at APEC Schools Tondo" took a proactive step to engage their audience by delving deeper into their credibility and persona.

Employing a personalized one-on-one introduction, they shared their individual backgrounds, experiences, and qualifications that equipped them to undertake this significant research endeavor.

During the live presentation, the researchers consciously adopted a formal and informative tone, providing a comprehensive understanding of their backgrounds and identities. 

02

Things Done Well

During the second livestream, notable improvements were observed, particularly in the preparedness and stability of technical aspects such as the microphone, camera, and internet connection compared to the initial broadcast. Additionally, the overall tone of the second session exuded a greater sense of professionalism and formality.

In anticipation of the live event held on April 18, 2024, the researchers diligently crafted three forms of promotional materials: invitations, posters, and a trailer, strategically disseminating information about the stream well in advance. This proactive advertising approach significantly bolstered viewership as audiences were provided ample time to mark their calendars. This marked a stark contrast from the first stream, which suffered from hasty and inadequate promotional efforts.

Technical glitches, such as laggy audio and video, were noticeably reduced during the second stream, resulting in a more coherent and visually polished presentation. This enhanced the overall viewing experience, allowing for clearer communication of the content.

Moreover, the second stream embraced a more formal and professional demeanor, a direct response to audience feedback from the initial broadcast. Addressing suggestions to minimize recreational elements and prioritize informative content, the researchers elevated the quality and relevance of the discussion.

In summary, the second stream saw significant enhancements in areas that had been highlighted as shortcomings during the first broadcast, culminating in a more polished and engaging viewing experience. 

03

Areas for Opportunities

The second stream has been acknowledged as an enhanced iteration of the first by respondents; however, it's important to note that not all respondents favored the second stream.

Opinions among survey participants were divided regarding their preference. Surprisingly, the majority ultimately favored the first stream. Their reasoning stemmed from finding it more enjoyable and entertaining.

This suggests that the transition to a more formal and professional atmosphere in the second stream may have inadvertently dulled the audience's experience.

Moreover, the preferred stream heavily depended on individual viewer priorities: whether they prioritized content or entertainment. Those valuing content likely favored the second stream, while those seeking entertainment found the first stream more appealing.

While the second stream retained some elements of amusement, it dialed back on the level of gaming present in the initial broadcast. Consequently, audiences leaned towards the first stream due to its inclusion of highly entertaining games.

In conclusion, further improvements to the second stream could involve striking a delicate balance between informative content and engaging entertainment, akin to the successful blend achieved in the first stream. 

04

Level of Engagement

The second stream significantly surpassed the first in terms of audience engagement. Impressively, it garnered a total of 38 reactions across various platforms, including both promotional materials and the live broadcast itself.

Furthermore, the level of interaction was elevated by the speakers' adoption of a personalized approach, engaging with the audience on an individual level. This resulted in a more intimate and interactive exchange of comments, enhancing the overall engagement experience.

However, while the second stream excelled in fostering direct communication with viewers, it fell short in terms of humorous interactions compared to its predecessor. The lively and humorous atmosphere of the first stream proved challenging to replicate, resulting in a slight decline in engagement in this aspect. 

05

Viewership

The second stream has notably eclipsed the reach of its predecessor, amassing a commendable total of 154 views when accounting for both the live broadcast and promotional materials. This substantial increase in viewership underscores a significant milestone in audience engagement and retention.

This heightened viewership not only signifies a broader audience base but also indicates a growing curiosity and interest among viewers, drawn to the stream by its compelling content. The sustained engagement of the audience throughout the stream and promotional period reflects a positive trend in viewer interest and engagement.

Moreover, this surge in viewership represents a promising trajectory for future streams. By effectively nurturing and catering to the evolving preferences of the audience, there exists a tangible opportunity to further bolster viewership and expand the reach of future broadcasts.

In essence, the positive growth in viewership witnessed with the second stream serves as a harbinger of potential success, heralding a wave of prospective viewers eager to engage with future content offerings. With strategic planning and continued focus on audience engagement, the platform is poised to capitalize on this momentum and cultivate a thriving community of loyal viewers.
